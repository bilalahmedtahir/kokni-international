About Us

M/S. Kokni International is a leading globally supplier-based organization. We started our business in 1979 under our family name with a dedicated and energetic team aiming for success.

Currently, we represent Southeast Asia and collaborate with world-renowned mills and suppliers from Canada, Sweden, USA, Austria, Germany, and more, to market their products worldwide.
